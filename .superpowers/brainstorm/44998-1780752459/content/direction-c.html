Visual DNA

Surfaces: Single deep background (#0B0F1A). Cards are pure #0B0F1A with 1px borders. Zero drop shadows except for floating elements.

Color: One signature accent (#5E6AD2 electric indigo) used in micro-doses — dots, section numbers, hover states. Status colors only when semantic.

Type: Inter for body, JetBrains Mono for codes/numbers/labels. Section numbering (01/, 02/) in mono.

Geometry: Zero radius on cards (sharp corners feel "considered"). Buttons: 8px radius. Tabular layout with subtle dividers, not floating cards.

Motion: 180ms. Quick, intentional, never luxurious. Hover states reveal accent color. Selection feels like a software tool, not a website.

Iconography: Ultra-thin, line-only. Tabler/Lucide style. 1.5px stroke. No filled variants, no glow.

Direction C · Studio Linear-Soft RECOMMENDED

Linear's product rigor + subtle warmth. Most distinctive within the "professional" space.

Pros

  • Most distinctive within "professional" — won't feel like a Linear clone
  • Editorial typography hierarchy feels premium without being cold
  • Section numbering (01/, 02/) creates rhythm and orientation
  • Sharp corners + thin dividers = "this is a precision tool"
  • Scales beautifully from phone to desktop
  • Performance: zero shadows, zero blur, zero gradients = fast everywhere
  • Information density that rewards returning users

Cons

  • Less immediately "wow" than Direction B (Aurora)
  • Sharp corners may feel "too austere" for some
  • Editorial typography requires careful balance — easy to overdo
